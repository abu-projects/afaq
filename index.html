About AFAQ ADI

AFAQ Digital Infrastructure (AFAQ ADI) stands at the forefront of digital transformation in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. As a specialized integration company, AFAQ ADI is committed to delivering state-of-the-art digital infrastructure solutions that are not only innovative but also inherently reliable and always ready to meet the dynamic demands of the modern business landscape.

Led by General Manager Mohamed Essawi, AFAQ ADI brings together a team of experts passionate about leveraging technology to solve complex challenges and drive progress. Our strategic location in Riyadh positions us perfectly to serve a diverse clientele across the region.

Our Services & Solutions

AFAQ ADI offers a comprehensive portfolio of IT solutions and services designed to meet the diverse needs of enterprises across various industries.

Data Center Solutions

End-to-end solutions, from design and implementation to management of traditional and smart data centers.

Network Infrastructure

Resilient and high-performance passive and active network infrastructures for seamless communication.

System Infrastructure

Integrated Solutions
